wit.ai Bot Engine Key Concepts

conversational platform

Jan 26

wit.ai released in this article in April 2016, their vision for conversational bots: an early beta of Bot Engine. Since they joined Facebook in 2014 months ago, the Wit community grew from 6,000 to 21,500 developers. 

They shared their key concept around building a bot engine, grouped around the following questions

- What is the user’s intent?
- How should the bot respond?
- What actions should it perform?
- What question should it ask?
